Lentil Dal

A delicious lentil dal to warm up your soul!

This recipe is very easy and will for sure charm your guests.

It pairs perfectly with naan bread.

INGREDIENTS

  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp
  • Indian curry - 1tbsp
  • Yellow lentils - 150g
  • Chopped tomatoes - 1 tin (approx. 400g)
  • Water - 2dl
  • Salt - 3/4 tsp
  • Pepper - a little
  • Plain yoghurt - 4 tbsp

HOW IT'S DONE

  1. Heat the oil in a pan.
  2. Roast the curry powder for approx. 1 min.
  3. Add lentils and tomatoes, pour in the water, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat.
  4. Simmer for approx. 15 mins., season, serve with yoghurt.
